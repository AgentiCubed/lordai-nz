The Great Library

Two organs being grown.

Beyond the crown there is research — the slow project of giving a mind something to actually stand on. Two sibling efforts, one frame: teach a model to care, and teach it to know where it is. Senses a machine is usually denied, given to it on purpose.

A sense of smell

A sense of space
